About Us

Wanna Play?

A community that connects people in every moment and place, starting from the city of Turin.

.

Wanna Play? was born from the idea of a group of student to provide a website to match people who want to play the same sport and allow them to share their passion.

  • The sport is the most common hobby in the world: it allows people to stay healthy, to have fun and to make new friends. For these reasons, WannaPlay? results in a useful website to prevent people from losing this habit.

    This platform has the purpose to solve the recurring problem to find all the teammates to play a team sport with. That is an issue for the ones that are new in town, but also when none of your mates are up for a match. But it is also very useful to all people that want to improve their own sport skills and do not know anyone of the same level to play with.
